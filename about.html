About Maison Vintique

Words by Our Founders

"At Maison Vintique, we don’t just sell decor – we curate timeless expressions of elegance. Our founders envisioned a space where classic British artistry meets modern sensibility. Every curve of our furniture and every hue of our décor is designed to narrate a story — your story. We believe that a home should feel like a living poem — layered, soulful, and ever-evolving."

Our History

Founded in 1952 by the late Mr. and Mrs. Sinclair, Maison Vintique was born from a quaint London atelier where the couple handcrafted bespoke pieces for aristocrats and collectors. What began as a passion project rooted in antique restoration soon blossomed into a legacy brand. Over decades, we preserved our old-world charm while evolving to embrace contemporary tastes, making our mark as storytellers of interiors.

Our Operating Chains

With over 30 artfully curated showrooms across the United Kingdom, each Maison Vintique outlet is a sensory journey. Designed to resemble miniature châteaus, our showrooms offer more than just products—they offer experiences. From scent to sound, texture to touch, we transport guests into a world where elegance reigns and inspiration lives in every corner.

Our Manufacturing Unit

Our workshop in the artisan-rich countryside of Cotswolds is where tradition meets technology. Here, local craftsmen and women breathe life into each creation using ethically sourced wood, sustainable techniques, and an eye for perfection. The scent of varnish, the rhythm of chisels, the joy of creation—it all culminates in products that tell timeless tales.

Research & Development

Our in-house R&D studio is where color palettes are born, and future trends are forecasted. Led by a team of visionary designers, Maison’s innovation hub experiments with form, texture, and finish, ensuring that we’re not just following trends — we’re shaping them. From AI-assisted designs to hand-sketched prototypes, our lab is a canvas of boundless creativity.

Meet Our Staff

We are a family of 200+ creatives, dreamers, and doers. From design consultants and artisans to marketing strategists and floor managers, each member of Maison Vintique brings their passion and personality into our collective mission—to elevate homes with warmth and wonder. We don’t just hire talent; we nurture it.

Life at Maison Vintique

From mood-board Mondays to chai chats in design lounges — life at Maison is beautifully chaotic. Our workspace blends creativity with calm, deadlines with laughter, and art with purpose. It’s not just a company; it’s a culture where inspiration is served fresh with morning coffee and where every idea has a place to bloom.
